Working! Screen:

Adult ADHD Questionnaire — 18 Items

1. Carelessness
Do you make silly mistakes?
Rush through your work?
Are you messy? Unable to find things?
2. Sustaining Attention
Do you have trouble remembering what was said or what you have read?
Does it take you longer to do things because you cannot keep your mind on the task?
3. Trouble Listening
Do you have to ask people to repeat directions to get something done?
Do people complain that you seem to daydream instead of listen?
When trying to remember, do you realize you've missed key parts of what was said?
4. Completing Tasks
Do you to forget to finish what you were doing?
Do you need a deadline to be able to finish your work?
Do you return to a room you've been in and realize you've left things out or opened?
5. Trouble Organizing
Do you have trouble breaking down tasks into orderded steps?
Do you have trouble with time management?
6. Avoids Lengthy Tasks
Do you have trouble with particularly long tasks or board games?
Do you tend to avoid them or feel better about shorter tasks?
7. Loses Items
Do you often misplace your keys, wallet or papers?
Are you constantly looking for things? Does this cause you trouble?
8. Easily Distractable
Do you get pulled away from things you are doing?
Do you start new tasks before several others are complete?
9. Forgetful in Daily Activities
Do you find it difficult to stick to a daily routine?
Do you forget to bring important work materials or assignments with you?
10. Fidgety
Can you sit still or do you need to be shaking your foot or otherwise moving? Are you always playing with your hair, clothing or items?
11. Trouble Sitting
When seated, do you often pop up to look at things or brouse? Would you rather be walking than sitting?
If you must stay seated do you need to constantly remind yourself as such?
