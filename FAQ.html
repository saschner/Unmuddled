What is Unmuddled?

Unmuddled translates business jargon into plain English for neurodivergent professionals who struggle with corporate communication. It processes emails, meetings, documents, and presentations to deliver:

  • Plain English summaries — Key points without the buzzwords
  • Jargon translation — "Synergy" → "working together effectively"
  • Tone analysis — Is this urgent? Collaborative? Directive?
  • Action extraction — What do I actually need to do?
  • Meeting context — Why this matters in the bigger picture
"Finally, a tool that makes business communication accessible without requiring me to decode every email." — ADHD professional, tech industry
